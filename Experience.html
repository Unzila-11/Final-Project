Camps and competitions:

  • Global Encounters Camp-Kenya (July 2024)

    • Global Encounters (GE) Camps are international programmes for Ismaili students, ages 15 to 17, from around the world.

    During the camps, participants live with their Ismaili peers worldwide, learn from exceptional educators and professionals, broaden their perspectives in thought-provoking and challenging courses, lead and participate in community service projects, gain cultural competencies and explore what it means to be a global citizen.

    During their time at GE Camps, participants collaborate with local community organisations on service projects that advance the well-being of communities. The programme includes exploration and fun through social activities, workshops and various excursions. (“The Ismaili”)

  • Meri Phechan Pakistan (Competition)

    • Meri Phechan Pakistan (MPP) was a competition arranged by the Girls Guide Pakistan, where I took part in the outdoor cooking competition. Even though our team did not win, it taught me efficient cooking techniques especially with limited resources.

  • 2024 Hult Future Leaders Challenge

    • The Hult Future Leaders Challenge (HFLC) is an 11-week, challenge-based, global competition for high school students (aged 15 to 18) that runs from September to December. Working in teams, students build, test, and pitch innovative business solutions, gaining mentorship from Hult professors and opportunities for college scholarships.
